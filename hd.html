Common Basilisk

Facts

  • Hatchling of the Common Basilisk can weigh up to 2 grams and are 38 to 43 mm long.

  • It is known to be run to 11km/h.

  • It is famous for its ability to run on water.

  • It can stay underwater for an hour.

  • It is also known as Jesus Christ Lizard.

  • It is mostly founded on the the Pacific side of the amazon basin.
